About

little bit about me

I'm an Engineer

I am Nandalal Parthiban, an Electrical and Electronics Engineer with a passion for cutting-edge technology and engineering innovation. I completed my Bachelor of Engineering in Electrical and Electronics at the University College of Engineering, Tiruchirappalli, and have since been actively involved in diverse engineering projects that push the boundaries of technology.

During my career, I’ve worked on numerous impactful projects, from developing Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs) with AI-powered vision systems to creating IoT-enabled embedded solutions that address real-world challenges. One notable project involved building a UAV integrated with an AI vision system for autonomous navigation and object detection, designed for applications like search and rescue and precision agriculture. This project allowed me to gain extensive experience in system integration, AI algorithms, and sensor fusion for real-time data analysis and decision-making.

Design

I specialize in designing robust systems that blend electronics and mechanics seamlessly. I have a keen interest in PCB design, IoT solutions, and prototyping for embedded systems. Through various projects, I have honed my skills in tools like KiCAD, Eagle, and Fusion 360, focusing on creating optimized circuits and mechanical designs for automation, robotics, and other innovative solutions.

Automation

With a deep passion for the Internet of Things (IoT), I’ve worked on building automated systems that leverage IoT protocols, communication technologies, and cloud-based solutions. My technical skills lies in using platforms like Arduino, ESP8266 and Raspberry Pi, allowing me to create intelligent systems that connect, communicate, and optimize processes. I'm committed to exploring new opportunities in smart systems, automation, and Unmanned Vehicles.

Innovation and Problem-Solving

Driven by a problem-solving mindset, I take pride in working on projects that require innovative solutions. My background in Electronics & Robotics, along with my hands-on experience, enables me to address technical challenges with creativity and determination. I am constantly learning new techniques and striving to apply them in real-world engineering solutions.
